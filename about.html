About ikorex

Our Story

Built by operators,
for operators.

ikorex started with a simple observation: the businesses losing the most money to inefficiency weren't lacking effort, they were lacking systems. Invoices chased by hand. Approvals stuck in email threads. Good people spending their week on work a machine should be doing.

Founded in 2024 by Yesudas Sebastian and Subin Peter, ikorex began by solving one problem well, bringing robotic process automation and WhatsApp-based workflows to Order-to-Cash and Procure-to-Pay operations that were bleeding time and money to manual processing. That focus earned trust across industries and it's what still shapes how we build today.

We're now based in Melbourne, working with businesses across Australia to turn operational complexity into automation that runs itself. The mission hasn't changed since day one: fewer manual hours, fewer errors, and operations teams who get their time back.
